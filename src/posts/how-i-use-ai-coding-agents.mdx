When I first started using Claude and OpenCode, I approached new projects a lot like I approach any new
software project. I established a carefully-planned foundation, started layering elements on top, and eventually worked
toward the goal I had in mind. A bit like building a pyramid.

After months of building, multiple new projects and mainenance on old ones, I've completely shifted my
perspective. Now, I think of building projects with agents like molding a piece of clay. I start out with a
blob that takes up the full "space" of the final result, and start refining it. It may only look roughly like my goal
at first, or even for a while, but with careful refinement, it eventually takes shape. Just like a lump of clay
is turned into a sculpture, my project over time gets refined and adjusted until it matches the vision in my head.
